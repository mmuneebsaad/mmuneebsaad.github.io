I received a bachelor’s degree from Gomal University, Dera Ismail Khan, Pakistan (2016), and a Master’s degree from COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus, Pakistan (2018) in Electrical Engineering. Currently, I am working towards a Ph.D. degree at Munster Technological University (MTU), Cork, Ireland. I am a recipient of the Risam scholarship award for my Ph.D. studies in computing at MTU. My research interests include utilizing AI for healthcare and leveraging the power of generative models to address the issue of limited data in medical imaging. My Ph.D. research is on developing novel approaches to improve the training of GANs for medical images. I have developed GAN-based methods to produce diverse and high-quality images that are adapted to the distribution of real image data. The generated images have helped to improve the AI-based classification of diseases like Pneumonia and COVID-19.
